I'm Lauren!

I started out my career working as an insurance Claim Specialist where I handled various financial lines claims in the areas of Employment Law, Errors & Ommissions, Crime and Cyber. As great as it was to work in that industry, I knew that there was something missing.

I've always had an interest in a variety of creative pursuits, and was craving that creativity in my work life. This is what brought me to Juno College! From the moment I printed my first "Hello World" on the page, I was hooked. (...)

I have a good eye for colour (and love working with it), and ensure all my designs and builds are crafted with a careful eye.

In my free time, I love being outdoors and going on hikes, as well as diving into a good book. I also enjoy listening to sociology or true crime podcasts, or working on creating new coding projects!

Overall, I'm someone who is always looking to learn and grow, both personally and professionally. I believe in the importance of connecting with others and using my skills to make a positive impact in the world.

I'm always eager to meet new people and explore new opportunities.
