In my free time, I love being outdoors and going on hikes, as well as diving into a good book. I also enjoy listening to sociology or true crime podcasts, and am always looking for new recommendations!

Overall, I'm someone who is always looking to learn and grow, both personally and professionally. I believe in the importance of connecting with others and using my skills to make a positive impact in the world.

I'm always eager to meet new people and explore new opportunities, so don't be a stranger!.
